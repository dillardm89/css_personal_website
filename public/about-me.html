About Me

Hello, I'm Marianne!

I am someone with a lifelong passion for learning and discovery. From a young age, I had my nose in a book more often than not, traveling to new worlds through stories. Over the years, this has grown into actual adventures as I love travelling to experience new people, cultures, and sceneries. When I’m home, I enjoy hiking on local trails while taking pictures along the way and practicing my handy-man skills doing DIY home improvement projects. My hunger for knowledge has also grown as I continue to read everything from sci-fi and fantasy novels to articles about the latest trends in business and technology.

On a professional level, I am a seasoned program leader with 10+ years of data analytics and process optimization experience. Professional focal points include team leadership, information-gathering, data analysis, reporting, mentorship, and change management. Delivering superior administration on the latter areas of expertise requires utilization of effective communication and negotiation skills, as well as the business acumen, systems analysis aptitude, and project/resource management abilities to support efficiency and maximize returns. I thrive when taking on new challenges, especially in the fast-paced world of business growth, and I’m always open to new opportunities to hone and expand my skillset.
